About Me

I am an IT Manager and Artist, living in Toronto, Canada, originally from India. I've been painting since I was 4 years old.

Over the years I've tried different mediums such as water color, acrylic, oil, crayons, pencil, charcoal and mixed media. I also love doing clay pottery and glass painting.

I've had various informal trainings in India and Canada, learning different techniques to express my passion for art. I've participated in group exhibitions in Kolkata, India and later in Brampton, Ontario.

I work for Mozilla, a not-for-profit organization that develops open source software products for the Internet. I enjoy traveling, capturing nature's magic with my lenses. I love to eat, cook and explore the gastronomic sides of the places I visit. Currently serving as an executive officer for my local Toastmasters club.
